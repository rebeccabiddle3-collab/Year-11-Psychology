How you're assessed

Assessments

Year 11 Senior Psychology has four Formative Internal Assessments (FIAs) — two per unit. There is no external examination in Year 11.

FIA1
Formative Internal Assessment 1 · Unit 1

Data Test

10%
About this task

A supervised examination using 2–4 unseen datasets (quantitative and/or qualitative). You read, interpret and respond to questions about the data using psychology concepts from Unit 1.

60 min + 5 min perusal Supervised · Closed book Unit 1
What's assessed
  • Apply — use calculations and identify features from data
  • Analyse — identify trends, patterns, relationships, limitations
  • Interpret — draw conclusions, deduce, infer, extrapolate
Tips
  • Practise reading scatter plots, bar charts and tables
  • Know how to calculate mean, median, mode
  • Write 3–5 sentence trend statements using data
📊 FIA1 Revision Site → 📄 Data Booklet (PDF)
FIA2
Formative Internal Assessment 2 · Unit 1

Student Experiment

20%
About this task

You plan and conduct a student-led investigation, then produce an individually written scientific report or multimodal presentation. Completed across approximately 10 hours of class time, based on a Unit 1 practical (e.g. Stroop effect, divided attention study).

Up to 2000 words ~10 hrs class time Unit 1 practical
What's assessed
  • Describe/Explain — psychology theory relevant to your investigation
  • Apply, Analyse, Interpret — your collected data
  • Investigate — plan, conduct and report a psychological inquiry
Report sections
  • Introduction · Method · Results · Discussion · References
FIA3
Formative Internal Assessment 3 · Unit 2

Research Investigation

20%
About this task

An individually written report or multimodal presentation based on a Unit 2 practical investigation you plan and conduct. Approximately 10 hours class time. More independently managed than FIA2.

Up to 2000 words ~10 hrs class time Unit 2 practical
What's assessed
  • All five objectives (Describe through Investigate)
  • Deeper independent analysis and evaluation expected
Possible investigation contexts
  • Emotive vs informative stimuli on heart rate
  • Stress & working memory correlational study
  • Cognitive performance across groups
FIA4
Formative Internal Assessment 4 · Units 1 & 2

Examination

50%
About this task

A school-developed written examination covering all content from Units 1 & 2. Includes short-response and extended-response items. This is your major assessment for Year 11.

Supervised exam Short & extended response Units 1 & 2
What's assessed
  • All five objectives across all 7 topics
  • SHE understanding embedded across questions
  • Short response: 1–4 mark questions
  • Extended response: 6–10 mark questions
How to prepare
  • Use the Tracking page to check syllabus coverage
  • Practise all key terms and researcher names
  • Complete past extended response questions under timed conditions
✅

No External Examination in Year 11

Unlike Year 12, there is no QCAA external exam for Units 1 & 2. All four assessments are school-based. Your results prepare you for the Year 12 external examination. Note: Year 11 assessments are called Formative Internal Assessments (FIAs) — they count towards your school record but not your ATAR.
